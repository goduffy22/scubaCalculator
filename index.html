Scuba Dive Calculator

This is an app that tells you whether the series of dives (on a particular day) is safe or not and what your pressure groups are after each of a series of dives. While scuba diving we have to be careful to monitor the levels of nitrogen gas absorbed into our bodies or we could be prone to decompression sickness which is not pretty and in some cases can be fatal. The levels are effected by the depth of your dive, the length of your dive and how long you rest at the surface for between each dive. To calculate these levels, PADI provides three tables which you can check out in the slider panel on the right. I hope this helps.

As a future project, I will add a reverse calculator or more functionality so that one can calculate the least surface interval time needed to make two consecutive dives of particular depth and time. Perhaps I will add a way to find how long the next dive can be or how deep the dive can go to depending on the previous dive/surface interval/depth or length of the current dive. i could even extend this to get ranges of surface intervals/length/depths or 3 or more consecutive dives so that you can play around with your dive plan and see what works.
